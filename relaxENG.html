Relax massage




Make an appointment today

Relax massage is a gentle, soft hand massage which is used to relax the organism by uniting the energy flow. Its goal is to bring nice care to a body in order to increase the overall health. It is used to prevent stress and exhaustion, and to relax the muscles, too. Relax masaža je blaga, prijatna ručna masaža kojom se postiže opuštanje organizma sjedinjavanjem protoka energije. Ima za cilj negovanje tela radi podizanja opšteg zdravstvenog stanja. Služi za prevenciju stresa i premora, kao i relaksaciju mišića.

Life in a fast lane causes unavoidable accumulation of stress hormones (cortisol) in organism. Increased level of this hormone can cause insomnia, headache, migraines, and even problems with digestive organs. Relax massage causes a good mood, it invokes relaxation and is considered to be an initial step for helping the organism to get rid of stress hormones.

Relax massage can be done fully, on the whole body, or partially (legs, arms, feet, back, torso, chest, neck and face).

Our recommendation is to feel the beauty of the full body relax massage. That way, you will feel rested and comfortable, and your msucles will be relaxed, with no tension. Except for that, our therapists will notice if some of your body parts is under greater tension or is injured. That way, you will know better which massages to choose.
