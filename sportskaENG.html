Sports massage




Make an appointment today

This massage is recommended to both professional and recreative people who do sports. There are a few types of sports massages.

Massage performed a few days after training or a long-term muscle work will prevent accumulating the milk acid, or will remove the very same if there is a bunch of it. Accumulated milk acid turns into calcifficates, which can cause muscle tension, or even physical pain.

There are sports massages whose role is to sanitate the most frequent sport injuries (throwers elbow, tennis elbow, swimmers shoulder, jumpers knee etc.). This massage is recommended even when there is no injury, but the muscle is tired, and massage will help it recover faster.

Often, sports massages are performed to prepare an athlete for competitions, matches etc. This massage relaxes the peripheral nerve system, and prepares the body for new exhausting efforts. Sports massages improve blood presence in muscles, so they can be better prepared to perform.

If you are an recreative athlete only, sports massage will suit you perfectly well, because milk acid accumulates even more in the muscles of non-professional athletes. If not cured on time, it can present a problem, and even cause pain.
