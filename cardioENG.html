Cardio


Cardio exercises are necessary for any efficient training program, not just for burning calories, but for the global wellbeing as well. By its definition, cardio training can consist of any exercises - running, cycling, swimming, treadmill running, stairs climbing, rope skipping - which all mantain rapid heartbeat for some time period.
With cardio exercises, you strenghten up your heart and lungs, and you reduce your heartbeat per minute ratio.

Who is it intended for?

This program can be attended by anyone, from inactive businessmen which just want to improve their stature and ease their office job, to experienced men who need more adrenaline in their life, and who also want to take care of both their appearance and health.
