About us


Teretana Pobednik offers you all fitness services in the very heart of the old city. We are offering you various types of training, massages, as well as our nutricionist advice. We possess all of the latest equipment for you to use, either by yourself or under coach's supervision. Our staff is very experienced at each segment of their trade, and is always there to offer assistance. Training can be scheduled by phone or via internet. Located in old Dorćol, Teretana Pobednik is easily accessible to anyone who wants to work on their looks and health. With its 400m2, we are the biggest gym in the whole city. Book your first appointment today!
