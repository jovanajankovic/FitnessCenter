Pilates


Pilates truly changes your body shape, its inner feeling and behaviour. It builds up strength without additional effort, creating a slim body with thight thighs and flat stomach. Pilates improves flexibility, strength and movement. It can even help with reducing back pain.

Who is it intended for?

Developed mostly from rehabilitation tehniques, Pilates is safe, suptile system of exercises, performed on a mat, and sometimes, with a big ball. Despite your age or form, Pilates will certainly be good for you.
