Gym


Teretana Pobednik offers you all fitness services on a central city location. We offer a large number of various types of training. We have the most modern equipment which is available for solo usage, or for usage undr the supervision of a professional coach. Our staff is experienced in all fields of fitness, and is always there to help. Training can be booked by phone or via internet. In the very heart of Dorcol, Teretana Pobednik is accessible to anyone who want to work on their appearance and their health. With its 400m2, we are the biggest in the city.

Teretana Pobednik is divided in a few areas, according to training programs, equipments and offered services.

Our gym awakens the winning spirit in you, with large number of different devices, located in three big halls.
